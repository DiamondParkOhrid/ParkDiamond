About us

Welcome to Park Diamond Apartments – where luxury meets adventure!

Experience comfort, excitement, and a touch of elegance at Park Diamond Apartments. Your gateway to a world of possibilities, nestled in breathtaking surroundings.

Since our inception in 2017, we've grown from six upscale apartments to a thriving destination. In 2022, we expanded to offer ATV rentals, added 15 more apartments, and introduced a vibrant market. Now, in 2025, we proudly provide 25 apartments, accommodating 60 guests in total.

Located in the complex Hotel Park, 3 km from the city of Ohrid. Park Diamond Apartments provides accommodation with lake & mountain view. A bicycle rental service, rent a car, paragliding, water activities, massages, jeep safari tours, catamaran tours are available at the reception. Each of our high luxury apartments offers great pleasure and are designed to satisfy all your needs.

Park Diamond Apartments

We offer 25 apartments suitable for individual stay, but also for organizing small events like seminars, team building groups, etc. Park Diamond Apartments has the capacity to accommodate up to 60 guests in different types of apartments.
